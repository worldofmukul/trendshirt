Who We Are

Founded in 2018, TrendShirt began as a small startup with a big vision - to revolutionize the way people shop for shirts online. What started as a passion project in a Mumbai apartment has grown into one of India's most trusted online shirt destinations.

At TrendShirt, we believe that clothing is more than just fabric - it's a form of self-expression. That's why we're committed to offering high-quality shirts that combine comfort, style, and affordability. Our carefully curated collections are designed to help you look and feel your best, whether you're dressing for the office, a night out, or just relaxing at home.

We partner with ethical manufacturers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Every shirt in our collection is crafted with attention to detail, using premium fabrics that stand the test of time.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to express their unique style through high-quality, affordable shirts that don't compromise on comfort or craftsmanship. We strive to make premium fashion accessible to everyone while maintaining ethical and sustainable practices throughout our supply chain.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted shirt brand in India, recognized for our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and innovative designs. We aim to redefine online shirt shopping by combining cutting-edge technology with personalized service to create an exceptional customer experience.
